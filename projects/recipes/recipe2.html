Background

There's nothing better than winding down after a long day with a warm, satisfying snack- that doesn't leave you feeling guilty or bloated. One evening, I felt as though I had not eaten enough throughout the day, and was on the hunt for something that could satisfy my sweet tooth. At the same time, I remained mindful of my nutrition goals, so I wouldn't allow myself to eat anything too unhealthy. I didn't want the usual sugary desserts, and a heavy meal was out of the question.

As I browsed through my pantry, I realized I had the perfect ingredients to create a bowl of cinnamon apple oats. The result? A nutritious late-night treat that feels like a big hug.

Why this recipe?

  • Quick to prepare (under 10 minutes!)
  • Perfectly balances sweetness with the savory flavor of cinnamon
  • Provides a healthy dose of protein, carbs, and fiber to fuel recovery and rest after a hard day of training or work

The Recipe

Cinnamon Apple Oats Recipe
Calories: 240 | Protein: 20g | Carbs: 38g | Fat: 2g
Ingredients Instructions
  • 30g oats
  • ~1/3 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 apple, sliced
  • 1/4 banana, sliced
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 scoop cinnamon-flavored whey protein powder
  1. In a small bowl, add the oats and the milk- enough so that the oats are singularly floating around in the bowl like cereal.
  2. Put the bowl in the microwave and heat it for about 50 seconds.
  3. Take it out of the microwave, then add the protein powder. The whey should mix harmoniously with the milky oats, and give them life.
  4. In a seperate plate, slice your apple and banana, and heat them for about 10 seconds in the microwave.
  5. Add the sliced fruits on top of the oats, along with a sprinkle of cinnamon, and a drizzle of honey. Enjoy!
Preparation Time: 10 minutes | Servings: 1 bowl
